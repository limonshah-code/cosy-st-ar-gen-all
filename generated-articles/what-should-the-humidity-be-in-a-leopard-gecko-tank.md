## Optimal Humidity for Your Leopard Gecko Tank

Creating the perfect home for your leopard gecko is a top priority for any responsible pet owner. We often focus on heat, lighting, and food. However, many new owners overlook a very important factor: humidity. Understanding **what should the humidity be in a leopard gecko tank** is vital for your pet's long-term health and comfort.

Leopard geckos come from arid and semi-arid regions. This means they do not need high humidity levels across their entire enclosure. However, they do need specific humid areas to thrive. Incorrect humidity can lead to serious health problems like respiratory infections or issues with shedding. This article will guide you through ideal humidity levels for your leopard gecko. We will cover how to measure humidity, how to adjust it, and how to spot potential problems. By the end, you will feel confident in providing the best humid environment for your beloved gecko.
