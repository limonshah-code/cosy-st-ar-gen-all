### Clear, Concise Answer to Main Query

Adjusting Delta shower valve pressure often involves checking the pressure-balancing spool or temperature limit stop inside the valve. For low pressure, inspect for clogs in the cartridge or showerhead. For high pressure, ensure the house's main water pressure is within limits, and adjust flow restrictors if present.
