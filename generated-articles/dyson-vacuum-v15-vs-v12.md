## Laser Illumination and Dirt Detection: Seeing the Unseen

One of Dyson's most innovative features in recent models is the laser illumination system. Both the Dyson V15 Detect and the Dyson V12 Detect Slim include this technology. This feature changed how people approach vacuuming. It makes hidden dust visible, ensuring a more thorough clean.

### Fluffy Optic™ Cleaner Head: Illuminating Dust

Both the V15 Detect and the V12 Detect Slim come with the Fluffy Optic™ cleaner head. This special head has a precisely angled green laser built into it. When you vacuum, the laser shines across the floor. It illuminates microscopic dust particles that you might not see with your bare eye. This makes the dust stand out on hard floors.

Seeing the dust helps you clean more effectively. You can easily spot dirty areas and make sure no spot is missed. This feature is particularly good for hard flooring like wood, tile, and laminate. It ensures a truly deep clean. You can confidently move on knowing you removed all the dust.

### Piezo Sensor: Quantifying the Dirt

Beyond seeing the dust, these vacuums also measure it. Both the Dyson V15 Detect and V12 Detect Slim have a Piezo sensor. This sensor is inside the vacuum. It listens for vibrations caused by dust particles entering the bin. It counts and sizes these particles as they pass through.

The data from the Piezo sensor displays on the LCD screen. You can see a real-time graph of the dust particles. This graph shows you what kind of dirt you are picking up. It also tells the vacuum to automatically adjust suction power. If it detects a lot of dust, it increases power. If the area is clean, it reduces power. This makes cleaning more efficient.

## Filtration Systems: Trapping Microscopic Particles

A vacuum's filtration system is vital for air quality. Both the Dyson V15 Detect and V12 Detect Slim vacuums use advanced filtration. They aim to trap dust and allergens inside the machine. This prevents them from returning to your home's air. A good filter system helps create a healthier living space.

### Whole-Machine Filtration: A Healthier Home

Both the Dyson V15 Detect and Dyson V12 Detect Slim feature whole-machine filtration. This means the entire vacuum system is sealed. Air flows through multiple filter layers before it leaves the machine. This design captures 99.99% of microscopic particles. It traps particles as small as 0.3 microns. This includes dust mites, pet dander, pollen, and bacteria.

The filter is washable and reusable. You can simply rinse it under water and let it air dry. This makes maintenance easy and keeps the vacuum working well. This high level of filtration is a significant benefit. It helps keep the air cleaner, especially for people with allergies or asthma. You can breathe easier knowing your vacuum is not just picking up dirt, but also purifying the air around you.

### Power Modes: Optimized Cleaning

Dyson vacuums offer multiple power modes. These modes allow you to adjust suction based on your cleaning task. Both the V15 Detect and V12 Detect Slim typically have three main modes:
*   **Eco mode:** This mode provides the longest run time. It is suitable for light cleaning and hard floors.

*   **Boost mode:** This mode offers maximum suction power. It is perfect for tough messes, deeply embedded dirt, or pet hair on carpets. Using Boost mode consumes battery life faster.
