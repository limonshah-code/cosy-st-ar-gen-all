## Essential Maintenance for Your Active Wash Washing Machine

Regular maintenance keeps your Samsung Active Wash washing machine running well. It also prevents odors and ensures clean laundry. I make it a point to clean my machine often. A clean machine performs better and lasts longer. Neglecting maintenance can lead to issues.

First, clean the detergent dispenser drawer regularly. Detergent and fabric softener residue can build up. This buildup can block dispensers or cause mildew. Remove the drawer if possible and wash it with warm, soapy water. Rinse it well and let it dry before putting it back.

Run a self-clean cycle every month or so. Your Samsung machine likely has a "Self Clean" or "Eco Tub Clean" cycle. This cycle uses hot water to clean the inside of the drum. It helps remove detergent residue, mildew, and bacteria. If your machine does not have this cycle, run an empty load with hot water and a cup of white vinegar or a washing machine cleaner.

Wipe down the inside of the drum after each use. Leave the lid open for a while after a wash. This allows air to circulate and the drum to dry. Drying prevents mold and mildew growth, which causes unpleasant smells. If you notice any weird smells or dirty spots, address them immediately. ## FAQ Section

### How do I use the Active Wash sink?

Pull down the washboard-like flap inside the drum. Fill it with water using the machine's water inlet. Add a small amount of detergent. Place your stained item on the textured surface and gently scrub. Once done, lift the sink to drain water and the item into the main drum for washing.

### Can I use laundry pods in my Samsung Active Wash machine?

Yes, you can use laundry pods. Do not put them in the dispenser drawer. Instead, place the pod directly into the empty drum *before* adding your clothes. The pod needs direct contact with water to dissolve properly during the wash cycle.

### What is the best cycle for active wear?

Many Samsung Active Wash machines have a dedicated "Active Wear" or "Sports Wear" cycle. If not, use the "Delicates" or "Hand Wash" cycle with cold water. This helps preserve the fabric and elasticity of performance clothing. Avoid fabric softeners with active wear.

### How often should I clean my Samsung Active Wash washing machine?

You should run a self-clean cycle at least once a month. Clean the detergent dispenser every few weeks. Always wipe down the drum and leave the lid open after each wash. This routine prevents mold, mildew, and unpleasant odors from building up.

### Why does my machine smell bad?

A bad smell often comes from mold or mildew buildup. This happens when moisture gets trapped inside the drum. Make sure to leave the lid open after washes to air it out. Also, run the "Self Clean" cycle regularly with a washing machine cleaner or white vinegar to eliminate odors.

### Can I wash very dirty items in the Active Wash sink?

Yes, the Active Wash sink is perfect for very dirty items. It allows you to pre-treat stubborn stains like mud, grease, or food spills. You can scrub the affected areas directly in the sink before the main wash cycle begins. This improves cleaning results for heavily soiled laundry.
