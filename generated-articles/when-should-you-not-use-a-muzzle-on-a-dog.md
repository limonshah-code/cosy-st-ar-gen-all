## When to Avoid a Dog Muzzle: Safety & Comfort First

Muzzles are often seen as tools for aggressive dogs. Some people might think they are a quick fix for behavior issues. However, knowing **when should you not use a muzzle on a dog** is critical for their health and happiness. Using a muzzle incorrectly can cause more harm than good. It is important to understand situations where muzzles are inappropriate. This article will explore key scenarios when you should avoid muzzling your dog. We will cover situations that threaten their physical and emotional well-being.
