## Lift Grease from Carpet: Baking Soda & Beyond

A grease stain on your beloved carpet can feel like a disaster. Whether it's from a dropped pizza slice or a cooking spill, these oily marks seem to cling stubbornly. I know how frustrating it is to see that dark spot spreading. Luckily, there are highly effective ways to get grease out of carpet, and baking soda is often the star player. This article explores how baking soda works its magic and introduces other powerful allies in your fight against stubborn grease. We will guide you through simple steps and give you practical tips. You can restore your carpet's clean look with confidence.

### Takeaway

*   Act fast when grease spills on carpet.
*   Baking soda is a top choice for absorbing fresh grease.
*   Blot, do not rub, to remove stains.
*   Combine methods for older or tougher grease spots.
