## Coway Air Purifier Filter Change: A Simple Guide

Do you want clean, fresh air in your home? Your Coway air purifier works hard to provide it. But even the best air purifiers need maintenance. *   Regular filter changes maintain clean indoor air.

## Step-by-Step Guide: Changing Your Coway Air Purifier Filters

Changing filters in your Coway air purifier is easy. Follow these steps for most Coway models. Always refer to your specific model's user manual if you have questions. Each model might have slight differences in design. This general guide helps you get the job done right.

### Locating and Removing the Pre-Filter

First, turn off your Coway air purifier. Unplug it from the wall outlet. This is a crucial safety step. Next, locate the front cover of your unit. Most Coway models have a removable front panel. Gently pull or unlatch the cover to remove it. You will see the filters inside. The pre-filter is usually the first filter you see. It is a mesh-like screen.

Carefully unclip or slide out the pre-filter. This filter often collects large dust particles and pet hair. It is usually washable. Take it to a sink or outdoors. Use a soft brush or vacuum cleaner to remove loose debris. Wash the pre-filter with lukewarm water if it is very dirty. Use a mild soap if needed. Rinse it completely. Let the pre-filter air dry fully before putting it back. A damp pre-filter can grow mold.

### Accessing and Replacing Carbon and HEPA Filters

Once the pre-filter is removed, you will see the next set of filters. These are typically the activated carbon filter and the HEPA filter. They are often stacked or combined. Gently pull them out. Note their orientation. You will insert the new ones in the same way. The activated carbon filter is usually thin and black. The HEPA filter is thicker and white or off-white.

Place the old filters into a trash bag. These filters capture a lot of pollutants. They are not washable and need proper disposal. Unpack your new filters. Make sure they are the correct type for your Coway model. Insert the new activated carbon filter first if they are separate. Then, insert the new HEPA filter. Ensure they fit snugly. There should be no gaps. These filters are essential for capturing fine particles and odors. Many people seek air purifiers with advanced filtration, like the [best air purifier for everything](https://www.cosyhomecreation.com/best-air-purifier-for-everything), which relies on these filter types.

### Resetting the Filter Indicator Light

After installing the new filters, put the front cover back on your Coway air purifier. Make sure it latches securely. Plug the unit back into the power outlet. The filter indicator light will likely still be on. You need to reset it manually. The reset button is usually small. It is often located near the filter indicator light or on the control panel.

Press and hold the reset button for 3-5 seconds. The light should turn off. If it does not, refer to your specific model's manual. Some models might require a different button combination. Successfully resetting the light means your Coway air purifier knows it has new filters. It will begin tracking the life of your new filters from this point. Now your unit is ready to provide clean air again.

## Troubleshooting Common Coway Filter Change Issues

Even with clear instructions, you might face small problems. Do not worry. Most filter change issues have simple solutions. Knowing what to do can save you time and frustration. We cover common problems people encounter.

One frequent issue is the filter indicator light not resetting. You press the button, but the light stays on. First, ensure you held the button long enough. Some models need a sustained press, usually 3-5 seconds. Make sure the unit is fully powered on and plugged in. Sometimes, simply unplugging and replugging the unit helps. If the light still stays on, check your manual. Some specific models have unique reset procedures. A tiny, recessed button might need a pen tip to press.

Another concern might be unusual smells after changing filters. This is rare with new, genuine filters. If you notice a strange plastic or chemical smell, check the filters. Ensure they are seated correctly. Sometimes, a new filter has a slight factory odor. This usually fades quickly. If the smell persists or is unpleasant, contact Coway support. There might be a manufacturing defect.

You might hear unusual fan noise after a filter change. This can mean a filter is not seated properly. A loose filter can vibrate. Turn off the unit and unplug it. Reopen the filter compartment. Gently push each filter into place. Ensure all clips or guides hold them securely. Close the cover firmly. If the noise continues, check for any debris. A small piece of packaging or dust might be trapped.

Lastly, some people find new filters do not fit. This almost always means you bought the wrong ones. Double-check your Coway model number. Compare it with the filter packaging. Different Coway models use different filter sizes. Always verify compatibility before purchasing. Returning incorrect filters is easier than trying to force them. Proper fit is crucial for effective air purification.

## FAQ Section

### How often should I change my Coway air purifier filters?
You should change your Coway air purifier's HEPA filter every 12-24 months. The activated carbon filter typically needs replacement every 6-12 months. The pre-filter is often washable and needs cleaning every 2-4 weeks. Your unit's filter indicator light is the best guide. High usage or poor air quality can shorten filter life.

### Can I clean Coway filters instead of replacing them?
Only the pre-filter on your Coway air purifier is typically washable. You can clean it with water and mild soap. The activated carbon and HEPA filters are not washable. Washing these filters can damage their structure and reduce their effectiveness. They must be replaced when they reach their end of life.

### What happens if I do not change my Coway air purifier filter?
If you do not change your Coway air purifier filter, its cleaning ability drops significantly. Dirty filters cannot trap pollutants, letting them recirculate in your home. This can worsen allergies and respiratory issues. The unit may also work harder, consuming more energy and potentially shortening its lifespan.

### Where can I buy replacement Coway air purifier filters?
You can buy replacement Coway air purifier filters directly from the Coway website. Many online retailers like Amazon, Best Buy, or home appliance stores also sell them. Make sure to purchase filters specific to your Coway model number. Look for genuine Coway filters or reputable aftermarket brands.

### Is it hard to change a Coway air purifier filter?
Changing a Coway air purifier filter is not hard. It is a straightforward process. Most models require you to remove a front panel, slide out the old filters, and insert new ones. The whole process usually takes only a few minutes. Follow the steps in your user manual or this guide for easy replacement.

### How do I know which Coway air purifier model I have?
You can find your Coway air purifier model number on a sticker. This sticker is usually on the back or bottom of the unit. The model number is also listed in your air purifier's user manual. Knowing your exact model ensures you buy the correct replacement filters for your unit.
