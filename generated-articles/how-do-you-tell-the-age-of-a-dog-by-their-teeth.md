## How Do You Tell The Age Of A Dog By Their Teeth?

Have you ever wondered about your dog's true age? Perhaps you adopted a rescue dog, and their birthdate is a complete mystery. Knowing your dog's age helps you provide the best possible care for them throughout their life. It guides crucial decisions about their diet, exercise routines, and preventative health screenings. The easiest and often most accurate way to estimate a dog's age is by looking closely at their teeth.
