## FAQ Section

### Q1: Can a firm mattress be comfortable for side sleepers?

Yes, a firm mattress can be very comfortable for side sleepers, especially with a pillow top. The firm base supports the spine and prevents sinking. The pillow top adds soft cushioning for pressure points. This combination offers both necessary support and plush comfort.

### Q2: What is the ideal firmness level for side sleepers?

The ideal firmness for side sleepers is generally medium-firm to firm. This level provides enough support to keep the spine aligned. However, it must also have a comfort layer or pillow top. This softer layer allows hips and shoulders to sink slightly, relieving pressure.

### Q3: Is a pillow top good for side sleepers?

Yes, a pillow top is often excellent for side sleepers. It adds a plush, contouring layer of comfort. This layer cradles the hips and shoulders, which are common pressure points for side sleepers. It helps prevent pain while maintaining the essential support from the core mattress.

### Q4: How long do firm pillow top mattresses last?

A quality firm pillow top mattress can last 7-10 years or even longer. The firm base helps maintain the mattress's structure. The pillow top's durability depends on its materials. High-density foams or latex in the pillow top can extend its lifespan.

### Q5: What's the difference between a pillow top and Euro top?

A pillow top is a distinct layer sewn on top of the mattress, creating a visible gap. A Euro top is sewn flush with the mattress edges, making it look more integrated. Both provide extra cushioning, but Euro tops often have denser materials and a sleeker look.

### Q6: Can a firm pillow top mattress help with shoulder pain?

Yes, a firm pillow top mattress can help with shoulder pain for side sleepers. The firm base supports the spine and prevents deep sinking. The plush pillow top cushions the shoulder, allowing it to sink just enough. This reduces direct pressure on the joint.
